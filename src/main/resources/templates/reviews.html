Welcome to Trina's Travels. Growing up in St. Louis, Missouri, I was never a big traveler. Other than the occasional visit to family down south, I never ventured far from St. Louis. As a young adult my initial travel excursions were all to urban areas of the United States. My only travel experience was within the United States. After settling into my career I decided I wanted to travel. After all, I had the job and my finances were stable. It was vacation time, right?

So for my first trip overseas in 2001, I went to London. That trip changed my life. It opened me up to the possibilities of the world. In London, I experienced other cultures, saw historic pieces dating back centuries, and met people from around the world. From that moment on, I was hooked on travel. Admittedly, London isn't that big a change from the U.S., but I was proud that I had taken the long 8 hour flight to see a different country. All I wanted to do was travel, see more of the world, and learn more about the people in it. My goal is to travel to every country and meet as many people as I can. So far I have visited over 10 countries and will share my experiences with you, so that you can motivate yourself to visit the same countries or live vicariously through my travels.
